RULES

Ping Pong will count up to your number starting from 1 with the following exceptions

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced by "pong"
  3. Numbers divisibel 15 are replaced by"pingpong"
